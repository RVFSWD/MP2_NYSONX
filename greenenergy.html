Green Energy Project

NYSONX is committed to helping the world transition to sustainable energy.

WE HELP PROVIDE SOLAR PANELS TO REMOTE AREAS IN THE PHILIPPINES.

Elevate rural living through decentralized energy, empowering communities for a sustainable future.

Promote energy autonomy by implementing mobile solutions in off-grid and underserved regions.

Our Community Recent Project.

Solar Energy Foundation

One notable example of a non-government project in a remote area is the Solar Energy Foundation's rural electrification project in Cebu. The foundation has installed solar systems in over 10,000 households in cebu, providing clean, reliable energy to people who previously had no access to electricity. This project has not only improved the quality of life for these households, but also created local jobs and promoted sustainable development in the region.

Liter of Light

In the Philippines, the Liter of Light project has been making significant strides in remote, off-grid communities. This innovative project uses recycled plastic bottles to provide solar lighting to homes that previously had no access to electricity. The bottles, filled with water and bleach, are installed into the roofs of houses and refract sunlight, providing as much light as a 60-watt bulb during the daytime. This simple, sustainable solution has already brightened up thousands of homes in rural Philippines and continues to make a difference in these remote communities.

Project Malasakit

In the remote areas of the Philippines, the Project Malasakit by Kara David has been making a difference in the lives of underprivileged children. This charity foundation provides scholarship grants to poor but deserving students, many of whom live in remote, hard-to-reach areas with limited access to quality education. The foundation not only covers school fees, but also provides for other needs such as school supplies and uniforms. Through this initiative, Project Malasakit has helped numerous children in remote communities to pursue their dreams and improve their future prospects.
