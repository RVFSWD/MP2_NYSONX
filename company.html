🌱 About Us

NYSONX began its journey as a startup, fueled by the passionate ideas of Kodego students, our visionary founder. What started as a spark of inspiration evolved into a mission to transform the landscape of energy and transportation in the Philippines.

🌴Green Energy Advocacy:

In the early days, some forward-thinking companies recognized the potential in our vision and invested in us. Their support became the catalyst for us to establish and promote green energy solutions. We believe in the power of collaboration, and these partnerships have enabled us to pave the way for a sustainable future.

🚘Driving Towards Thousands of EV Stations:

At the core of our mission is the ambition to bring about a paradigm shift in transportation. We aspire to see thousands of Electric Vehicle (EV) stations across the Philippines, making clean and efficient transportation accessible to all. By strategically placing these stations, we aim to redefine the way people commute and contribute to a significant reduction in carbon emissions.
